In a near-future world where rogue nations and corporate warlords have plunged the globe into open conflict, one classified unit stands between order and total chaos — STRIKE VECTOR. Handpicked from the world’s elite military branches, the Vanguard deploys cutting-edge vehicles and weapons technology to neutralize threats no conventional army can handle. But their greatest enemy isn’t on any map. General Scorch and the Iron Veil are a shadow organization hell-bent on seizing control of the world’s most advanced military hardware — and they’ll burn everything down to get it. Every mission is a race. Every battle raises the stakes. And the line between victory and catastrophe is razor thin.
